Mission Statement

Paint-A-Bait is a family owned business that is passionate about making fishing fun, interactive and exciting for children and adults. Paint-A-Bait encourages creativity and teamwork by giving young fishermen the opportunity to create their very own lure.

What is Paint-A-Bait?

It starts with an actual blank fishing lure that can be painted with our very colorful and lifelike “paints”. Lures can be painted to resemble real fish, or they can be whacky, never seen before patterns. The possibilities are endless. Regardless, the end result is a unique, one of a kind, work of art. We believe every child will be “hooked” for life once they experience the thrill of catching a fish on the very lure that they personally brought to life. Paint-A-Bait is committed to the future of fishing and donates a portion of every sale to help less fortunate children experience the thrill of fishing.
